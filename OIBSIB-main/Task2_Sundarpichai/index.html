About Sundar Pichai

Sundar Pichai is the CEO of Alphabet and Google. Born in India, he joined Google in 2004 and quickly became one of the most influential leaders in the tech industry.

He played a key role in launching products used by billions of people worldwide including Chrome, Android, and Google Drive.
